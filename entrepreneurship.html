Entrepreneurship & Personal Development Lesson Plan

40 hours Nelly Entrepreneurship

Week 1 - Business Ideation and Evaluation

Overview

  1. Introduction to Business Ideation 20 mins
  2. Understanding the Process of Generating Ideas 30 mins
  3. Business Idea Evaluation 30 mins
  4. Common Traits of Entrepreneurs 15 mins
  5. Q&A and Closing 10 mins

Week 2 - Business Funding

Overview

  1. Overview of Business Funding Options 20 mins
  2. Business Loans 25 mins
  3. Venture Capital 25 mins
  4. Friends and Family 20 mins
  5. Crowdfunding 20 mins
  6. Q&A and Closing 10 mins

Week 3 - Building a Startup Team

Overview

  1. Building a Co-founding Team 20 mins
  2. Splitting Equity Among Co-Founders 30 mins
  3. Team Work 30 mins
  4. Characteristics of effective Team Work 15 mins
  5. The Benefits of Effective Team Work 15 mins
  6. Q&A and Closing 10 mins

Week 4 - Curation of Pitch Deck and pitching

Overview

  1. Understanding the Elements of a Pitch Deck 30 mins
  2. Pitch Practice 30 mins
  3. Q&A and Closing 30 mins

Week 5 - Launching a Product into the Market

Overview

  1. Introduction to Launching a Product 15 mins
  2. Launching an MVP 20 mins
  3. Getting the First 100 Customers 20 mins
  4. Reiterating Product Development with Early Adopters' Feedback 15 mins
  5. Q&A and Closing (10 mins) 10 mins

Week 6 - Written Communication in Professional Settings

Overview

  1. Introduction to Written Communication 20 mins
  2. Understanding the Effect of Relationship in Communication 30 mins
  3. Principles of Business Letter and Memo Writing 30 mins
  4. CV Writing 20 mins
  5. LinkedIn and Cover Letter Writing 30 mins
  6. Q&A and Closing (10 mins) 10 mins

Week 7 - Employability Skills and Personal Development

Overview

  1. Understanding Employability Skills (20 mins) 20 mins
  2. Introduction to Personal SWOT Analysis 20 mins
  3. Developing a Personal Development Plan 30 mins
  4. Q&A and Closing 10 mins

Week 8 - Leadership Skills

Overview

  1. Introduction to Leadership Skills 20 mins
  2. Strategies for Developing Leadership Skills 30 mins
  3. Leadership in Business and Corporate Environments30 mins
  4. Overcoming Common Leadership Challenges 30 mins
  5. Q&A and Closing 10 mins
